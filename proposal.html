PURPOSE:




This website serves as a medium of fun and knowledge, especially in the field of outer space and heavenly bodies. It offers a little escape from a monotonous day into the world beyond Earth. You can get some basic information about heavenly bodies or even get to know what NASA found on your birthday!!




THEME:




The website and its constituent webpages are themed as per the looks of the universe. The choices of font, content, images and even styling is in accordance with the theme. The aim was to help the users dive into the world of outer space, with the help of colours which are pleasant and comfortable to look at.




TECHNOLOGIES USED:




HTML, CSS and Javascript are the three pillars supporting the framework of the website. HTML laid the foundation with the necessary content on the website, such as the text, buttons and forms. CSS added styling to the content. And Javascript gave the required functionality for an ultimate user experience. Other than this, the website also contains a REST API for an exciting experience.




ASTRONOMY PICTURE OF THE DAY (APOD) API:




The National Aeronautics and Space Administration, U.S.A., offers an interesting API through which, by entering a date, one can access the 'picture of the day' captured by NASA. The aim behind using the API is to spark curiosity and interest among users in the field of outer space, and also provide them with an engaging activity.
